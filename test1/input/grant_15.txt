description: This preceptorship provides a senior level resident one-on-one training with a practicing traumatologist (preceptor) at an AO North America Center or with a member of AONA in basic fracture management. Additionally, this preceptorship provides an individual with in-depth study for improved general fracture care. Note: This program is not intended to fund a set rotation for a residency program. 

The preceptorship is available to senior residents who plan to practice general orthopedic surgery, and who want in-depth training in basic fracture care. A given center is only eligible for one Senior Resident Rotation Preceptorship per year. Formal approval from the applicant's residency director and the host-training program are both required.
